Languages

Coding languages, also known as programming languages, are tools used by developers to create software, websites, and applications. They serve as a means of communication between humans and computers, allowing programmers to give instructions to the machine. There is a wide variety of coding languages, each with its own syntax, rules, and purpose. Some popular coding languages include Python, known for its simplicity and versatility; JavaScript, which powers dynamic web content; Java, favored for its platform independence; C++, used for system and game development; and HTML/CSS, fundamental for web design and structure.
